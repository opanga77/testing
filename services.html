LIQUID SOAP

This is one of the innovative ideas that can make you start-off your own bussiness.

How to make Liquid Soap locally.

We just need the following
  • 500mls of sodium lauryl ether sulfate
  • 500g of sodium chloride /normal salt or iodized salt.
  • 500mls of cocodiethanolamide.
  • 20gms of a water-soluble dye.
  • 40mls of benzalkonium chloride.
  • 20mls of fragrance to Perfume the soap.
  • 20 liters of distilled water.
  • Medium sized basin.
  • 500ml pet bottles for packaging and storing.
  • Gas stove and Pot for boiling water,Measuring pots and cups to measure water and ingredients.
  • Plastic ladle to mix.

Here are the mixing Procedures:
  • Heat 5 liters of distilled water in a pot to boil for up to 15 minutes.
  • Make sure you use distilled water to avoid any contamination.
  • Using tap water will lead to bad odor and will also affect the outcome of your homemade soap.
  • Dissolve ungerol iodized salt in the boiled water and mix with a ladle in a basin.
  • Make sure that it mixes well before you can move to the next step.
  • Mix the detergent in the basin with the boiled water until the iodized salt is completely dissolved.
  • Add in the other remaining ingredients and stir until properly mixed and dissolved.
  • Let the mixture cool until the point bubbles subside.
  • Add the 15 liters of cold water to the mixture until well mixed.
  • Once cooled you can put them in the PET bottles. You can brand them as you want.
  • N/B:Be careful when choosing a brand as this may affect your marketing strategy.

CHARCOAL BRIQUETTES

Every waste counts,for there are a lot of ways to make the re-used right from animals waste to plants

You can make briquettes from sugarcane wastes,used papers/boxes,cowdung e.t.c.

Here is how you can make Chacoal briquettes from Cowdung.

This is a very simple process that is very eco friendly and helps save our forests.

We just need the following materials;
  • Waste dust from Chacoal
  • Cowdung
  • Water

Here are the simple procedures:
  • Mix the cowdung with the Chacoal dust.
  • Add some water to the mixture.
  • Mix the contents to make a dough.
  • Shape the dough into fist size balls using your hand.
  • Put the balls on a wide surface that fits and dry them in the sunlight.
  • After completely dry,the Chacoal is ready for use.

GARLIC SPRAY/MOSQUITO REPELLANT

Looking for this?

This is how to make it

We just need the following materials;
  • 1 tablespoon of mineral oil.
  • 5-6 garlic cloves.
  • 1 teaspoon of lime juice.
  • 2 cups of water.

    • Here are the simple procedures:
  • Mince the garlic cloves and add a tablespoon of mineral oil to this,
  • Soak overnight and strain the garlic,
  • Collect the infused oil and add lime juice and water to it,
  • Spray this solution on the plants around your home.
  • You can also spray this solution on the exposed parts of your body.
  • This should be done atleast two to three times a day.

How this solution works;

Garlic contains a compound called allicin that exibits a strong repelling action against mosquitos. Additionallly,garlic also has a strong aroma that repels mosquitos.It's also widely used as a natural insect repellant. The acidic nature of lemon juice enhance the repelling properties of garlic.
